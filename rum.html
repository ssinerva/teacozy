Spiced Rum Tea

If you're looking for a good kick in the back of your throat, this blend of ginger and allspice is for you! This blend is perfect for adult taste, and makes a great iced tea. You can add sweetener to further accent the punchy or drink it with sugar for a more crisp cup.

Serving Suggetion:

2 ounces double-strength* rum tea, chilled 2 ounces barrel-aged rum ½ ounce allspice dram 2 dashes Angostura bitters
